# 70_sales

Sales-supporting technical fact sheets. Documents here exist to
support sales conversations with evidence-based, technical
content. They are not marketing material and do not contain
hype, taglines, customer testimonials, or invented claims.

Allowed content:
- Feature-by-feature technical fact sheets
- Boundary and data-flow summaries derived from
  `../30_compliance/gdpr/data-flow-map.md`
- Compliance summaries derived from `../30_compliance/`

Forbidden content:
- Pricing
- Roadmap commitments
- Customer names or logos
- Invented certifications

Every document MUST cite the source-of-truth document under
`20_architecture/`, `30_compliance/`, or `50_runtime/`. If the
source-of-truth does not yet exist, the sales document is
deferred until it does.
