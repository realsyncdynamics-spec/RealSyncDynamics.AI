# Phase 6 Execution Monitoring & Progress Tracking

**Framework:** Real-time visibility into operational excellence execution  
**Duration:** 12 months (July 2026 - July 2027)  
**Owner:** Engineering Leadership  
**Update Frequency:** Weekly (retrospectives) + Monthly (reviews) + Quarterly (strategic)

---

## Executive Overview

Phase 6 execution success depends on continuous monitoring of three parallel workstreams:

1. **Weekly Retrospectives** - Team learning and continuous improvement
2. **Operational Metrics Dashboards** - System health and performance visibility
3. **Engineer Onboarding Program** - Team capability and skill development

This document provides leadership with standardized tracking mechanisms, success metrics, and decision frameworks for the 12-month operational excellence journey.

## Escalation Procedures

### When to Escalate (Red Status)

**Immediate Escalation (Same Day):**
- Engineer is blocked on critical path (can't proceed with onboarding)
- Dashboard deployment behind >3 days
- Production uptime drops below 99%
- Error rate exceeds 2% for >30 minutes
- Retrospective attendance <75%

**Next Business Day Escalation:**
- Dashboard metrics not updating (data staleness >1 hour)
- Engineer progressing slower than expected (1-2 weeks behind)
- Improvements not being completed (0 completions by Week 3)
- Team satisfaction drops below 7/10 (monthly survey)

**Weekly Review Escalation:**
- Consistent yellow status (2+ weeks)
- Trend data showing negative trajectory
- Process effectiveness concerns from team
- Resource constraints impacting execution

### Escalation Matrix

| Severity | Discoverer | Action | Timeline | Receiver |
|----------|-----------|--------|----------|----------|
| Critical | Anyone | Slack urgent | Immediate | Engineering Lead |
| High | Team Lead | Email + chat | Today | Engineering Lead + CEO |
| Medium | Engineering Lead | 1:1 meeting | This week | Team Lead + Owner |
| Low | Tracking system | Weekly review | Friday | Leadership team |

### Decision Framework (Red → Green)

When a task/metric turns red:

1. **Diagnose** (24 hours)
   - What is the root cause?
   - Is this a process issue, resource issue, or external blocker?
   - What data supports this?

2. **Decide** (48 hours)
   - Can we fix it? (Mitigation option)
   - Do we need to delay? (Timeline adjustment)
   - Do we need more resources? (Allocation change)
   - Is the goal itself flawed? (Scope adjustment)

3. **Communicate** (48 hours)
   - Inform all stakeholders of status and plan
   - Update tracking and timelines
   - Assign new owner/resources if needed
   - Schedule follow-up checkpoint

4. **Execute** (Ongoing)
   - Monitor progress toward green status
   - Weekly check-ins until resolved
   - Document lessons learned
