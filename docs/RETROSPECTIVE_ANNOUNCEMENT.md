## What to Expect

This is our first weekly retrospective as a team. It's a **learning opportunity, not a criticism session**. We'll celebrate wins, identify challenges, and commit to 3 actionable improvements this week.

**Format:**
- Each person shares: what you worked on, your status (on track/behind), any blockers, and one win
- Team discussion: What was challenging this week? What surprised us?
- Improvement brainstorm: Top 3 improvements we can implement immediately
- Action items: Clear owners and due dates for each improvement

## Agenda (60 minutes)

1. **Welcome & Context** (5 min)
   - Frame as learning opportunity
   - Psychological safety: all feedback welcome
   - Outcome: 3 actionable improvements

2. **Status Round-Robin** (10 min)
   - Each person: Project / Status / Blocker / Win
   - 1-2 minutes per person

3. **Production Deployment Review** (8 min)
   - What went well with launch?
   - Quick highlights on team communication and monitoring

4. **Challenges & Learnings** (15 min)
   - What was hard this week?
   - What surprised us?
   - Document impact level (critical/high/medium/low)

5. **Quick Wins & Improvements** (15 min)
   - Identify top 3 improvements
   - Each improvement: problem → solution → owner → due date
   - Focus on what's doable this week/next

6. **Action Items & Commitments** (5 min)
   - Confirm owners understand scope
   - Confirm timelines are realistic
   - Commit to action items in #engineering

7. **Announcements & Q&A** (2 min)
   - Next retrospective: Friday July 25 @ 4 PM UTC
   - Any urgent questions?
