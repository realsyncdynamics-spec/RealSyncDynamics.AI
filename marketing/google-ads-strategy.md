## Tracking & Conversion-Setup

### GA4 Events (vor Ad-Launch implementieren!)
```js
// Free-Tool genutzt
gtag('event', 'audit_completed', {
  tool_name: 'dsgvo_audit',
  score: 73,
  findings: 8
});

// Pro Sign-up
gtag('event', 'sign_up', {
  method: 'professional',
  value: 149,
  currency: 'EUR'
});

// Stripe Purchase
gtag('event', 'purchase', {
  transaction_id: '{stripe_session_id}',
  value: 149,
  currency: 'EUR',
  items: [{item_name: 'Professional', item_id: 'prof_monthly'}]
});
```

### Google Ads Conversion-Imports aus GA4
1. `audit_completed` → Conversion „Free-Tool-Use" (Wert €5)
2. `sign_up_professional` → Conversion „Trial-Start" (Wert €30)
3. `purchase` → Conversion „MRR" (Wert dynamisch)

### Enhanced Conversions
- E-Mail-Hash bei Sign-up an Google senden (gehasht, EU-compliant)
- DSGVO-Hinweis: nur nach Consent
- → 15–25% bessere Conversion-Attribution durch iOS-/Cookie-Restrictions

## Budget-Allocation (Monat 1)

| Campaign | €/Tag | €/Monat | Erwartung |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 — Pain-Keywords (Search) | €15 | €450 | 60 Klicks/Tag · 6–9 Free-User · 0,3 Pro |
| 2 — Competitor (Search) | €5 | €150 | 8 Klicks/Tag · 0,8 Free-User · 0,1 Pro |
| 3 — EU AI Act (Search) | €5 | €150 | 6 Klicks/Tag · 0,6 Free-User · 0,1 Pro |
| 4 — Performance Max | **€0** | **€0** | (erst Monat 2 nach Daten-Sammlung) |
| 5 — Remarketing | €5 | €150 | 30 Impressionen/Tag · 1 Re-Visit · 0,1 Pro |
| **Gesamt** | **€30** | **€900** | **~17 Free-User · 0,6 Pro/Monat** |

**Break-Even-Math:**
- 1 Pro = €149 MRR × 18 Mo Avg-LTV = €2.682
- CAC bei 0,6 Pro/Mo & €900 Spend = €1.500/Pro
- **LTV/CAC = 1,79** → profitabel, aber tight

**Skalierungs-Trigger (Monat 2+):**
- CAC < €500 in einem Ad Group → Budget verdoppeln
- CAC > €1.500 → Pause + Re-Iteration
- Search Impression Share < 50% bei profitablen Keywords → Budget hoch
- Performance Max ab Tag 31 dazuschalten (sobald Conversion-History vorhanden)
