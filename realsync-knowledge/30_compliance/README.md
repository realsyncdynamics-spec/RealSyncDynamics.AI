its own sub-folder. No claim of compliance — operational mapping
only.

|------------|------------------------------------------|
| `gdpr/`    | GDPR / DSGVO data-flow, retention, DSAR  |
| `ai-act/`  | EU AI Act inventory and risk classification |

`../90_templates/compliance-template.md`.
